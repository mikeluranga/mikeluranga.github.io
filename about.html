More About me.

After finishing my Ph.D. in Economics in the end of 2013 I started working as a data analyst in the eKnow IT project. In this project I am responsible for analysing those best practices on logistics that are leading the transportation system towards a modal shift.

During my research experience I realized that I have a passion about data. In this regard, I have devoted myself to improve my programming skills and gain more knowledge in Machine Learning algorythms by taking specialized courses.

Besides data science, economics and coding I love photography. You can check some of my latest pictures in my Tumblr.
